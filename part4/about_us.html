About The Project

HBnB is a web platform designed to help users discover different accommodations in Riyadh, including hotels, resorts, and chalets in a modern and easy way. The website allows users to browse places through cards containing images, service details, and reviews. After logging in, users can select their preferred place and write a review based on their experience. The project focuses on providing a simple and clear user experience with a contemporary design that reflects local identity, making the search for accommodations comfortable and fast.
